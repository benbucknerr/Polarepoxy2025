Concrete flooring Preperation

Concrete flooring preparation is a critical step in ensuring the durability and longevity of epoxy coatings. At Polar Epoxy, we use advanced techniques such as diamond grinding and shot blasting to remove imperfections, contaminants, and old coatings. This process creates a clean, textured surface that allows the epoxy to bond effectively, ensuring a flawless and long-lasting finish for your floors.
